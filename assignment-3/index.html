Fruit Facts

  • Apples are rich in antioxidants that help reduce the risk of chronic diseases.
  • Bananas can help regulate blood sugar levels and improve digestive health.
  • Oranges are packed with vitamin C, boosting your immune system.
  • Strawberries are low in calories but high in nutrients, including vitamin C and manganese.
